Brief Introduction

INTPs are free creatives, fast-moving, esoteric scholars who are easily distracted and endlessly creative without a break in their minds. Their preference for and deep understanding of abstract concepts leads them to deliberately engage in any work that is creative and challenging. They are calm, easygoing, rational, and mostly slow.

With the Introverted, Intuitive, Thinking, and Judging personality traits, these thoughtful tacticians love perfecting the details of life, applying creativity and rationality to everything they do. Their inner world is often a private, complex one.
