E
Extroverts

are energized by people, enjoy a variety of tasks, a quick pace, and are good at multitasking

I
Introverts

often like working alone or in small group, prefer a more deliberate pace, and like to focus on one task at a time

S
Sensors

are realistic people who like to focus on the facts and details, and apply common sense and experience to solve problems

N
Intuitives

prefer to focus on possibilities and the big picture, easily see patterns, and seek creative solutions to problems

T
Thinkers

tend to make decisions using logical analysis, objectively weigh pros & cons, value honesty, consistency and fairness

F
Feelers

tend to be sensitive and cooperative, and decide based on personal values and how others will be affected

J
Judgers

tend to be organized and prepared, like to make and stick to plans, and are comfortable following most rules

P
Perceivers

prefer to keep their options open, like to be able to act spontaneously, and like to be flexible with making plans
