Brief Introduction

The main characteristics of INTJ are high calmness and concentration, and careful thinking. Some books refer to INTJs as specialists, because most INTJs focus on one area and then become very sophisticated, understanding every aspect of a specialty and becoming a cult figure. Personally, I think INTJ is also one of the most persistent and persevering type 16 personalities.

INTJ can best be summed up as "independent". Independence is what motivates their prospects. If possible, INTJ wants everyone to be independent. This sense of independence may conflict with their simultaneous need to control their surroundings.
