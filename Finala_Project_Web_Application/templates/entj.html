Brief Introduction

It's no accident that ENTJs are natural leaders. They love to boss people around and set plans and directions for everyone. Because they instinctively act and behave from a high perspective, they embody many of the basic qualities of a leader: enthusiasm, vision, objectivity, and conscientiousness.

An ENTJ with these traits can hardly stay in power. Because they are witty and obedient, in most cases others are happy to be told what to do, and come to depend on them. ENTJ, with his talent for leadership, will never let his subordinates down.
