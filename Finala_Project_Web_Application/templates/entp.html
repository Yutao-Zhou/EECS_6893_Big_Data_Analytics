Brief Introduction

There are many personality types of ENTP, and it is difficult to describe them in one word. Some people call them inventor, resourceful, and adventurer. In a word, they are unwilling to be lonely, optimistic, humorous, curious, fond of thinking, argumentative, creative, and unwilling to do things they think boring. I prefer to call them Nobita's "robotic cats".

ENTP is a person with the Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking, and Prospecting personality traits. They tend to be bold and creative, deconstructing and rebuilding ideas with great mental agility. They pursue their goals vigorously despite any resistance they might encounter.
